Activating Play Mode…

Save the Play Mode Activators Community to your phone’s Home Screen so that you can open it like an app!

Tip: Place it next to an app you tend to open automatically (like Instagram or email) — so when you're tempted to scroll, you can scroll through the community instead and find meaningful inspiration or connection.

On iPhone (Safari):
  1. Tap the Share icon (the little square with the arrow pointing up out of it) at the bottom of your screen
  2. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  3. Tap "Add" to save it with the Play Mode icon
On Android (Chrome):
  1. Tap the ⋮ menu (top-right corner)
  2. Choose "Add to Home screen"
  3. Tap "Add" to confirm

Already saved it? Tap here to enter now.
